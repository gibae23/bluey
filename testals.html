About Us

Fast and Reliable Locksmith Services – Customer Satisfaction Guaranteed

At Affordable Locksmith Solutions (ALS), customer service is our top priority. We are a 24-hour locksmith service covering all suburbs in Sydney. All of our employees are certified master locksmiths and proud members of the Master Locksmiths Association.

We specialize in all aspects of locksmithing, including:

  • Safes
  • Residential properties
  • Commercial buildings
  • Automotive locks
  • Emergency lockouts
  • Access control systems
  • Restricted key systems
  • And much more

Our team provides fast, reliable, and competitively priced services, with a strong focus on 100% customer satisfaction.

Based in Sydney, we handle jobs of all sizes — from small residential tasks to major commercial fit-outs. We have successfully completed projects in some of Sydney’s largest buildings, so no job is too big or too small for ALS.
